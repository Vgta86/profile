About Me

Product Development, Prototyping & Production - Hardware and Firmware

I am a driven Product Developer & Manufacturer with a talent for integrated development environments and hardware design. I can bring your ideas from concept to mass market using efficient methodologies and proven design philosophies. As an electronic engineer and commercial PCB manufacturer, I fully develop IoT devices, AC/DC Power Controllers, EV Motor Controllers, Remote Surveillance Devices, Handheld LoRaWAN Devices, Industrial Automation Machines, and much more. I also personally develop firmware and embedded systems. I design and produce PCB/PCBA using Altium Designer and EasyEDA, and assemble the mass products with IP-rated enclosure. If you would like to inquire about my services and completed products, please feel free to contact me anytime.
